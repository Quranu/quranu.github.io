# Ayah 6

Sesungguhnya Tuhanmu itulah yang Maha Mengetahui lagi Maha Bijaksana.
EN: "Your Lord has thus blessed you, and has given you good news through your dream. He has perfected His blessings upon you and upon the family of Jacob, as He did for your ancestors Abraham and Isaac before that. Your Lord is Omniscient, Most Wise."
