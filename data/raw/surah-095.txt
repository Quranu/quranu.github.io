FOOTNOTE_BM: *95:1-3 Buah tin, zaitun, Sinai dan Mecca berkemungkinan simbolik bagi masing masing Adam, Isa, Musa, Ibrahim dan Muhammad. Lantas, merupakan semua agama.
FOOTNOTE_EN: *95:1-3 The fig, olive, Sinai, and Mecca possibly symbolize Adam, Jesus, Moses, Abraham, and Muhammad, respectively. Thus, all major religions are represented.
